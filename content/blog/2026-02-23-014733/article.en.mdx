### What Does This Mean for Us?

I believe this AI wave impacts each of us on multiple levels.

**For workers**: As AI models become smarter, tools like GPT-5, capable of handling complex tasks, will automate many repetitive, low-skill jobs. At the same time, they will also create more new positions requiring "human-AI collaboration." You might need to learn how to better collaborate with AI, turning it into your super assistant. Just as you once learned to use Office software, now you'll need to learn to master AI tools.

**For businesses**: Companies that effectively leverage AI infrastructure and the latest models will gain a significant competitive advantage. However, if your company merely "wraps" large language models (colloquially known as "LLM Wrappers") or functions as an "AI aggregator" without true technological depth and specific application scenarios, you might be in danger.
