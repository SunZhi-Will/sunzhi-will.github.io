#### üí° Energy Forecasting and Physical AI: AI Makes the Physical World Smarter

In the energy and infrastructure sectors, AI's potential is equally immense. Dr. **Mark Sinclair**, a doctoral researcher at the University of New England in Australia, confirmed that utilizing **transformer models** (the same AI architecture as ChatGPT) can significantly improve the accuracy of electricity price predictions, even reducing forecast errors by nearly half. This is highly significant for countries like Australia with volatile electricity markets. Accurate electricity forecasting helps batteries charge at the right time, generators make optimal decisions, and reduces defensive measures by grid operators, ultimately lowering costs for consumers and increasing renewable energy efficiency.

And this year's **CES 2026** Consumer Electronics Show, held from January 6-9 in Las Vegas, explicitly marked the birth of **"physical AI."** NVIDIA CEO **Jensen Huang** noted in his keynote speech that "the ChatGPT moment for physical AI has arrived," emphasizing that AI will transform from virtual tools into active participants capable of understanding, reasoning, and acting in the real world.

NVIDIA unveiled several new models and technologies for physical AI at CES 2026, such as the **Cosmos** platform and **Isaac GR00T**. The Cosmos platform is likened to "Android for robots," offering open-world foundation models trained on 20 million hours of robot data, capable of creating physically simulated training scenarios through synthetic data generation technology. Isaac GR00T focuses on humanoid robot control, handling joints, movement, and motion. **Boston Dynamics** also showcased a real-time demonstration of its Atlas humanoid robot and announced a partnership with **Google DeepMind** to integrate the Gemini Robotics AI model into Atlas, enabling robots to learn new behaviors more quickly. Furthermore, OpenAI plans to launch its first physical AI consumer hardware product later in 2026, in collaboration with former Apple design chief **Jony Ive**.
