### What Does This Mean for Us?
For us ordinary folks, this news might sound a bit distant, but in reality, it's quietly influencing our lives and work.

**First, changes in job opportunities**. As I mentioned earlier, AI isn't just a "tool"; it's now becoming a "thinking colleague" and even has the potential to become "the boss." OpenAI's Codex-Spark can double programmers' efficiency, which is certainly a good thing. But as AI gets smarter and handles more cognitive tasks, those repetitive, rule-based entry-level white-collar jobs are truly in jeopardy. Think about it: if AI can write reports faster and better than you, will a boss still pay to hire new staff? This could leave many recent graduates facing the predicament of a disappearing "apprenticeship period."
