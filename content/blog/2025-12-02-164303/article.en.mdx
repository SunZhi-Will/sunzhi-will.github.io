## Website Design Philosophy

### Simple and Elegant

The design of this website pursues simplicity and elegance. I believe good design should not overshadow the content but should let the content be the protagonist. Therefore, I chose a simple visual style, allowing readers to focus on the content itself.

### User Experience First

Whether it's page loading speed, responsive design, or interactive experience, I hope to provide visitors with a smooth and comfortable browsing experience. Technology should serve users, not the other way around.

### Continuous Improvement

This website will continue to be updated and improved. I will continuously optimize the website's design and functionality based on user experience and feedback. This is a living project that will grow and evolve over time.

## Future Plans
